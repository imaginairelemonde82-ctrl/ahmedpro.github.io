About

Who We Are

Passionate About
Cinema

Founded in 2015, Cinéma Magnifique was born from a deep love of the seventh art. We believe that great cinema has the power to transform perspectives, ignite emotions, and connect people across cultures and languages.

Our platform brings together over 12,000 films from 150 countries, curated by a passionate team of cinephiles, critics, and filmmakers. From Hollywood blockbusters to intimate arthouse gems, we celebrate the full spectrum of cinematic expression.

History

Our Journey

The Beginning

Cinéma Magnifique was founded by three film enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform that truly honored the art of cinema. Starting with 500 curated titles.

2015

International Expansion

We expanded our catalog to include films from 50 countries and launched support for 12 languages, reaching our first million users.

2017

Award Recognition

Named "Best Streaming Platform" by the International Cinema Federation. Our editorial team won the Critics' Choice for best film curation.

2019

4K Revolution

Launched our 4K Ultra HD library with Dolby Vision and Atmos support. Partnered with major studios for exclusive director's cut releases.

2021

4 Million Members

Reached 4 million active members worldwide. Launched our mobile apps in Arabic, French, and English with full RTL support.
