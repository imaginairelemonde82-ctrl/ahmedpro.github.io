About Cinema

Founded in 2015, Cinéma Magnifique was born from a deep love of the seventh art. We believe great cinema has the power to transform perspectives, ignite emotions, and connect people across cultures.

Our platform brings together over 12,000 films from 150 countries, curated by a passionate team of cinephiles, critics, and filmmakers.

History

Our Journey

The Beginning

Cinéma Magnifique was founded by three film enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform that truly honored the art of cinema. Starting with 500 curated titles.

2015
2017

International Expansion

We expanded our catalog to 50 countries and launched support for 12 languages, reaching our first million users.

Award Recognition

Named "Best Streaming Platform" by the International Cinema Federation. Our editorial team won the Critics' Choice for best film curation.

2019
2024

4 Million Members

Reached 4 million active members worldwide. Launched trilingual apps with full RTL Arabic support and 4K Ultra HD catalog.
