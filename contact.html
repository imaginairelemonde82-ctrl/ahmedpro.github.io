Help

FAQ

How do I create an account?
Click on "Join" in the navigation bar. Account creation is free and takes less than 2 minutes.
What subtitle languages are available?
We offer subtitles in over 40 languages including English, French, Arabic, Spanish, German, Japanese, and more.
Can I download films for offline viewing?
Yes! Premium subscribers can download up to 25 films for offline viewing on mobile devices.
How do I submit a film for consideration?
Use the "Film Submission" option in the contact form above. Our team reviews all submissions within 30 days.
Is content available in 4K quality?
Over 3,000 titles are available in 4K Ultra HD with HDR10 and Dolby Vision support on compatible devices.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your account settings with no penalties. Access continues until the billing period ends.
