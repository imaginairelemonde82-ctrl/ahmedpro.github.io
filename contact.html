Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on the "Join" button in the navigation bar and fill in your details. Account creation is free and takes less than 2 minutes.
What languages are subtitles available in?
We offer subtitles in over 40 languages including English, French, Arabic, Spanish, German, Japanese, and many more.
Can I download films for offline viewing?
Yes! Premium and Business subscribers can download up to 25 films for offline viewing on mobile devices.
How do I submit a film for consideration?
Use the "Film Submission" option in the contact form above. Our curation team reviews all submissions within 30 days.
Is the content available in 4K quality?
Over 3,000 titles are available in 4K Ultra HD with HDR10 and Dolby Vision support on compatible devices.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of your billing period with no penalties.
